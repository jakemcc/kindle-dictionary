Usage

On modern (as of 2013/06/23) e-ink Kindle's running recent software all you should need to do to use this dictionary is to get it onto your Kindle. You can do this by using the USB connection or by emailing the dictionary to the email address associated with your Kindle. Once it is on your Kindle you simply look up words like you would any other word in a dictionary. Older versions of the Kindle may require more work.

Details

The content of this dictionary was sourced from Wikipedia. It was marked up with appropriate metadata to allow it to be used as a Kindle dictionary.
